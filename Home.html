How Figorate Works

User Onboarding

User signs up on the Figorate platform. Completes a comprehensive onboarding questionnaire covering fitness goals, health status, dietary preferences, and any specific health conditions. Optionally, the user can opt for genetic testing by providing a DNA sample.

Personalized Assessments

Based on your genetic results, our experts conduct personalized assessments to understand your specific health needs, fitness goals, and nutritional requirements.

Tailored Health Plans

Receive customized fitness plans, nutrition programs, and wellness solutions designed specifically for you. Harness the power of data-driven insights to optimize your health and well-being.

Expert Guidance and Support

Get expert guidance and support from certified trainers, nutritionists, and healthcare professionals throughout your wellness journey. We're here to help you achieve your goals and live your healthiest life.
